Iced Teas Recipes

Iced Lemon Tea

What you will need:

  • Tea : 1 teaspoon loose black/green tea leaves
  • Cold Water : 1.25 liters [5.25 cups]
  • Saucepan
  • Sugar : 1/3 to 1/2 cup
  • Lemon : 3 tablespoons lemon squeeze
  • Strainer

Instructions:

Step 1: Boil cold water.

Set the saucepan over a medium heat and bring the water come to a boil.

Step 2: Put the tea into the saucepan

Add the tea leaves to the boiling water. Let it steep for 5 minutes. Add your sugar and mix well.

Step 4: Cover the teapot and steep for 4-5 minutes

Black tea is best steeped between 3 and 5 minutes. I like to steep this for 4 minutes. If you like a stronger cup of tea, brew more tea instead of steeping longer. Longer brewing can make your tea bitter.

Step 5: Add lemon squeeze into the tea

Add the lemon squeeze into the tea and mix well. Let the tea cool down to room temperature and stir the mixture well with a spoon

Step 6: Strain the tea into glass jar for storage

Strain the tea into a glass jar and store it in the refrigerator. Serve chilled with ice cubes and lemon slices. Enjoy!

Iced Green Tea

What you will need:

  • Tea : 2 teaspoons loose tea, or 2-3 tea bags
  • Cold Water : 1 cup [8 fl. oz]
  • Saucepan
  • Ice : 2-3 cups

Instructions:

Step 1: Boil cold water.

Since tea is made with just water and tea leaves, use good quality water. Use filtered water instead of tap water if possible. I recommend using an electric kettle with a temperature setting so you can be more accurate with the water temperature. Set it to 190Â°F. Boil some extra water to warm up the teapot.

Step 3: Put tea leaves or bags into the teapot.

To make tea stronger, always add more tea instead of brewing it for longer. A longer brew time will only make your tea bitter, not stronger.

Step 4: Cover the teapot and steep for 3-5 minutes

To make tea stronger, always add more tea instead of brewing it for longer. A longer brew time will only make your tea bitter, not stronger.

Step 5: Strain the tea leaves and pour cooled down tea into a cup

Add ice cubes into the cup of tea. Enjoy your iced green tea!

Iced Blueberry Tea

What you will need:

  • Tea : 1 teaspoon loose tea, or 2-3 tea bags
  • Cold water : 4 cups
  • Blueberry : 1 cup
  • Sugar : 1/3 cup
  • Lemon : 2 teaspoons
  • Potato masher
  • Strainer
  • Pitcher

Instructions:

Step 1: Add ingredients and bring it to a simmer

The process begins by making a blueberry simple syrup. 1 cup blueberries, 1/3 cup honey or sugar, 1/3 cup water, and 2 teaspoons lemon juice should be added to a small saucepan. Over medium heat, bring the mixture to a simmer. Stirring consistently, use the spoon (you might also find a potato masher helpful) to crush the blueberries. Once the sugar has fully dissolved and blueberries are all crushed, reduce the heat to low and simmer for 10 minutes.

Step 2: Strain the mixture

Strain the mixture into a pitcher and let it sit for a while.

Step 3: Bring 4 cups of water to a boil and add tea leaves/bags

Bring 4 cups of filtered water to a boil on the stovetop (or in a large glass measuring cup in the microwave). Remove from the heat and add the black tea. Steep the tea in the water for five minutes. Strain it with a fine mesh strainer.

Step 4: Mix everything together and enjoy!

Mix the blueberry simple syrup, brewed black tea, and remaining water by pouring them into a 68-72 ounce pitcher filled with 1 cup ice. Serve/pour over additional ice in glasses. Garnish as desired with fresh mint leaves, sliced lemon, and adding a few fresh blueberries to each glass. Enjoy!
